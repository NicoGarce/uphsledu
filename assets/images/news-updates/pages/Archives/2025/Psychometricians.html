Congratulations to our new Psychometricians!

Published by University of Perpetual Help System Laguna on June 27, 2025

We’re proud to announce that UPHSL achieved a 100% passing rate in the JUNE 2025 Psychometricians Special Professional Licensure Exam. The exam was held last June 6–7, 2025, in international testing centers across UAE, KSA, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, Singapore, and Taiwan.

This exam is part of PRC’s initiative to provide professional licensure opportunities to Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs), in line with PRC Resolution No. 1034 (s. 2017), Republic Act No. 8981, and Executive Order No. 835.

Your hard work and dedication truly paid off — we’re proud of you!
